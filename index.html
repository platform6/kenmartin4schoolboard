Ken Martin 
for Merrimack School Board
 

Hello!

My name is Ken Martin and I am running for a position on the Merrimack School Board. My family and I moved to Merrimack in 2017 and we are here to stay.

I am the parent of a 1st grader at Mastricola Elementary School on top of being a taxpayer and resident of our great town. I am currently the Director of Instrumental Music at Winnacunnet High School in Hampton, NH and have been for the past 6 years. Prior to Winnacunnet High School, I taught elementary and middle school band and general music classes in the Goffstown School District for 9 years. I have been actively involved in public schools in New Hampshire in some aspect since I was 5 years old, whether as a student, educator or parent. I currently serve on the executive board for the New Hampshire Music Educators Association and have served on a number of educational committees and task forces for the New Hampshire Music Educators Association as well as the New Hampshire Department of Education.
